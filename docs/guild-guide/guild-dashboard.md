# Guild Dashboard

Once your guild is verified, the **Guild Dashboard** becomes your command center. It's where you manage members, coordinate endorsements, track proposals, monitor impact, and communicate internally. This guide explains every section of the dashboard and how to use it effectively.

---

## Accessing the Dashboard

1. Log in to CivicNotes.
2. From the main navigation, click **"Guilds"**.
3. If you belong to multiple guilds, select the guild you want to manage from your list.
4. Click **"Guild Dashboard"** (or the shield icon) to enter.

---

## Members Tab

The **Members** tab is where you manage who belongs to your guild and what roles they have.
```markdown

| | Alex | Admin | Jan 2025 | Today | [···] |
| | Jamie | Member | Feb 2025 | 2 days ago | [···] |
| | Priya | Endorsement| Mar 2025 | Today | [···] |
| | +3 pending | Manager | | | |
| +----------------------------------------------------------------+
| |
| PENDING REQUESTS (2) |
| • Taylor (@taylor) · District 3 · requested Apr 20 |
| [Approve] [Decline] [Message] |
| • Jordan (@jordan) · District 5 · requested Apr 19 |
| [Approve] [Decline] [Message] |
+------------------------------------------------------------------+
```

### Member Roles

| Role | Privileges |
|------|------------|
| **Member** | Participate in discussions, vote on internal decisions, suggest endorsements. |
| **Endorsement Manager** | Review and approve endorsement suggestions; cast the guild's endorsement vote. |
| **Admin** | Manage members (approve/remove), edit guild profile, create announcements, represent guild in Guild Council. |
| **Founder** | Special badge; can transfer ownership, delete guild (rare). |

### Actions

- **Invite** – Send an invitation link via email or shareable URL.
- **Export List** – Download member data (names, contact info if shared) for external coordination.
- **Approve/Decline** – Handle pending membership requests.
- **Message** – Send a direct message to an individual member.
- **Change Role** – From the member's action menu (···), adjust their role.
- **Remove Member** – Only for severe Code of Conduct violations or inactivity (use sparingly).

### Proposals

- **Create a New Proposal** – Click "New Proposal" and choose to start from scratch or from the Regenerative Action Library.
- **Proposal Statuses**:
  - **Draft** – Still being edited; not yet public.
  - **Internal Vote** – Guild members are voting on whether to adopt it.
  - **Submitted** – Officially submitted to the relevant government body.
  - **Adopted/Acknowledged/Addressed** – Tracked via Promise Tracker.
- **Collaborative Editing** – Admins can invite members to co‑edit draft proposals.

### Endorsement Queue

When a member clicks **"Suggest Guild Endorsement"** on any CivicNote, it appears here. Endorsement managers and members can:

- **View the note** and its context.
- **Discuss** in a threaded comment section attached to the endorsement suggestion.
- **Vote** (if your guild uses voting for endorsements). Thresholds are set in guild settings.
- **Approve** – Moves the note to "Endorsed Items" and adds the guild badge to it.
- **Decline** – Removes from queue (with optional feedback to suggester).

### Endorsed Items

A historical list of everything your guild has officially endorsed, with current status (if applicable – e.g., "Acknowledged by Council").

## Tips for Effective Dashboard Use

### Daily/Weekly Rhythm

| Frequency | Suggested Actions |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Daily** | Check Pending Actions; approve/decline membership requests; review new endorsement suggestions. |
| **Weekly** | Scan Proposals & Endorsements queue; vote on internal decisions; post an announcement if needed. |
| **Monthly** | Review Analytics; celebrate wins in discussions; plan upcoming events. |
| **Quarterly** | Prepare for Guild Council meeting; update charter if needed. |

### Roles and Delegation

- **Don't let one person do everything.** Assign at least two admins and one endorsement manager to share the load.
- **Rotate roles** periodically to build shared capacity.
- **Use discussions** for strategic decisions, not just announcements.

### Integrating with External Tools

- Many guilds also use Signal, Discord, or WhatsApp for real‑time chat. You can link to these in your guild profile or announcements.
- For document collaboration, consider linking to a shared folder (e.g., Google Drive) – but keep sensitive information off public platforms.

### Keeping Members Engaged

- **Welcome new members** personally with a direct message.
- **Celebrate small wins** – every endorsed note or petition signature matters.
- **Ask for input** regularly on endorsement decisions.
- **Host events** – both online and in‑person – to build community.
