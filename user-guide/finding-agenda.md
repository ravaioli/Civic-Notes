# Finding Agenda Items

The heart of CivicNotes is the public meeting agenda. Here you'll discover what your local government is planning, see what your neighbours are saying, and decide where to add your voice. This guide walks you through finding, filtering, and understanding agenda items.

---

## Accessing the Agenda List

1. From any page, click **"Agenda"** in the top navigation bar.
2. You'll see the **Upcoming Meetings** view, showing all meetings synced from official sources (e.g., Legistar, Granicus) for the next 30 days.
```markdown
+------------------------------------------------------------------+
| UPCOMING MEETINGS [Filter ▼] |
| |
| TODAY |
| • Planning Commission · 6:00 PM · 8 items · Φ 72 |
| |
| TOMORROW |
| • City Council · 7:00 PM · 12 items · Φ 78 · 34 notes |
| • School Board · 5:30 PM · 5 items · Φ 65 · 12 notes |
| |
| THIS WEEK |
| • Zoning Board of Appeals · Wed · 4 items · Φ 81 |
| • Parks & Rec Committee · Thu · 6 items · Φ 69 |
| |
| [View Calendar] [Browse All Items] [Saved Items] |
+------------------------------------------------------------------+
```


Each meeting card shows:
- **Meeting name and time**
- **Number of agenda items**
- **Φ‑Score** for the meeting (aggregated from its items)
- **Note count** (if any)

## Filtering and Searching

Use the filter panel to narrow down meetings and items that matter to you.

### Available Filters

| Filter | Options | What It Does |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| **Date Range** | Today, This Week, This Month, Custom | Shows meetings within selected period. |
| **Government Body** | City Council, School Board, Planning Commission, etc. | Limits to specific bodies. |
| **Neighborhood/District** | District 1, District 2, etc. | Shows items that affect your area (based on location data in notes). |
| **Φ‑Score Range** | Thriving (71–100), Stable (41–70), Critical (0–40) | Finds items with high or low civic engagement. |
| **Intent** | Support, Oppose, Question, Proposal, Witness | Filters items by the dominant intent of notes. |
| **Guild Activity** | Active, Endorsed | Shows items where guilds are engaged. |

To apply filters:
1. Click the **"Filter"** button (top right of agenda list).
2. Select your criteria.
3. Click **"Apply Filters"**.

Your active filters are shown above the results; click **"Clear All"** to reset.

## Understanding an Agenda Item Page

Click any agenda item to open its dedicated page. This is where the conversation happens.

### Key Sections

| Section | What You'll Find |
|---------|------------------|
| **Header** | Meeting name, date, item number, official description, and links to full text/staff reports. |
| **Φ‑Score & Sentiment** | Current civic health score and breakdown of notes by intent (support/oppose/etc.). |
| **Sentiment Over Time** | A small chart showing how activity and opinion have evolved. |
| **Community Notes** | The feed of CivicNotes on this item. You can filter by intent, sort, and see endorsements. |
| **Related Petitions** | Active petitions tied to this item. |
| **Promise Tracker** | Summary of promises linked to this item (proposed, verified, etc.). |

## Taking Action from an Agenda Item

From any agenda item page, you can:

- **Leave a CivicNote** – Scroll to the note composer, type your comment, choose an intent, and post.
- **Suggest Guild Endorsement** – On any note you appreciate, click "Suggest Guild Endorsement" to bring it to your guild's attention.
- **Start a Petition** – Click "Start Petition" (near the top) to create a petition based on this item.
- **Propose a Promise** – After watching the meeting, click "Propose Acknowledgment" on your note or another's to create a promise record.
- **Watch/Attend** – Click "Watch Live" or "Get Reminder" to receive a notification before the meeting.
- **Share** – Use the share button to send the item via email, social media, or copy link.

## Saving Items and Setting Reminders

Never miss an item you care about.

### Save an Item
Click the **"Save"** (bookmark) icon on any agenda item or card. Saved items appear in:
- Your dashboard under **"Saved Items"**
- A dedicated **"Saved"** tab in the agenda view

### Set a Reminder
1. On an agenda item page, click **"Remind Me"**.
2. Choose when to be notified:
   - 1 day before the meeting
   - 1 hour before
   - At meeting start
3. Notifications arrive via your chosen method (in‑app, email, or push).

## Tips for Effective Agenda Browsing

- **Check Φ‑Scores first** – High scores indicate active, healthy discussion; low scores might mean an issue needs attention.
- **Use the "Near You" filter** – See items that directly affect your neighborhood.
- **Follow guilds** – If a guild's focus matches yours, check their endorsed items for quality proposals.
- **Set up saved searches** – From the filter panel, click "Save This Search" to create a custom view you can revisit anytime.
- **Explore past meetings** – Use the date filter to look at archived items and see how issues evolved.
